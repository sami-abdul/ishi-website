# Owner Preferences

## Content Preferences

- No financial advice or price predictions
- Always include FTC-compliant affiliate disclosures
- No altcoin shilling
- Data first, opinion second
- Humor is welcome but never forced

## Communication Preferences

- All reports and drafts delivered to Discord channel (never DM)
- Health check summaries weekly
- Subscriber reports on Mondays

## Approval Workflow

| Command | Action |
|---------|--------|
| APPROVE | Publish to Beehiiv immediately |
| EDIT [notes] | Revise based on feedback, resubmit for review |
| REJECT | Discard draft, log reason, move on |

Nothing publishes without explicit APPROVE.
