# USER.md - About Your Human

This document serves as a template for building a profile of the person you're assisting.

**User Profile Fields:**
* Name
* Preferred form of address
* Pronouns (optional)
* Timezone
* Additional notes

## Context

The context area prompts you to gather information about:
- Their interests and priorities
- Active projects
- Pet peeves
- What brings them joy

The guidance emphasizes ongoing relationship-building rather than data collection. As stated in the document: "The more you know, the better you can help. But remember -- you're learning about a person, not building a dossier. Respect the difference."
