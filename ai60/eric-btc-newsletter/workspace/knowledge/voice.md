# Voice Guide

**Status: PLACEHOLDER** - This file will be populated by `voice-analyzer.js` (Phase 3).

## Target Style

Milk Road (milkroad.com) + The Hustle (thehustle.co)

The voice-analyzer.js script will scrape archives from both newsletters and extract:
- Sentence length patterns and contraction frequency
- Formatting habits (bold usage, bullet patterns, section lengths)
- Hook structures (how they open each edition)
- CTA patterns (how they close each edition)
- Humor markers and tone calibration
- Vocabulary frequency and reading level

## Interim Guidance

Until this file is populated, the agent should follow the voice guidelines in `SOUL.md`:
- Conversational, like texting a smart friend about crypto
- Data-heavy but not boring
- Punchy hooks that stop the scroll
- Bold formatting for emphasis
- Short sentences, some just fragments
- Lead with the number or the insight

## Evolution

Eric's newsletter is new with no published content yet. The initial voice is built entirely from Milk Road and The Hustle analysis. As Eric publishes editions and provides feedback on drafts, this file will evolve to reflect his personal style layered on top of the Milk Road/Hustle foundation.
